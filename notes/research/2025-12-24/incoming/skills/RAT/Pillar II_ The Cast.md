\# Pillar II: The Cast

\> \*\*SCOPE:\*\* Character profiles, movement theory, and scalability.  
\> \*\*USE WHEN:\*\* Writing character action, defining personality, or creating new cast members.

\#\# 1\. ROOSTER (The Agitator)  
\*   \*\*Archetype:\*\* High Energy / High Failure. The "Angry/Proud" Vector.  
\*   \*\*Visuals:\*\*  
    \*   \*\*Silhouette:\*\* Angular, Triangular, Sharp 'V' construction.  
    \*   \*\*Features:\*\* Orange/Brown feathers, Red furrowed brows.  
    \*   \*\*Material:\*\* Harder edges, distinct separation.  
\*   \*\*Motion Lexicon (Active Verbs):\*\*  
    \*   \*Keywords:\* Snaps, Jerks, Stomps, Coils, Vibrates, "Explosive Transitions."  
    \*   \*Timing:\* \*\*Snappy POSE-TO-POSE.\*\* Fast action followed by rigid "Reaction Holds."  
\*   \*\*Transformative States (Body Horror):\*\*  
    \*   \*Toasted-Black:\* Covered in soot/tar, eyes glowing green.  
    \*   \*Plucked:\* Pink skin, no feathers, shocked expression.  
    \*   \*Flattened:\* Compressed into a 2D disc shape.

\#\# 2\. TERRY (The Pacifist)  
\*   \*\*Archetype:\*\* Low Energy / High Success. The "Stupid/Lucky" Vector.  
\*   \*\*Visuals:\*\*  
    \*   \*\*Silhouette:\*\* Rounded, Pear-shaped, Soft S-curves.  
    \*   \*\*Features:\*\* White/Blue feathers, Wall-eyed pupils (Googly Eyes).  
    \*   \*\*Material:\*\* Soft, rounded edges.  
\*   \*\*Motion Lexicon (Passive Verbs):\*\*  
    \*   \*Keywords:\* Drifts, Slides, Yawns, Sways, "Happens to be there."  
    \*   \*Timing:\* \*\*Fluid Rhythm.\*\* He arrives at the perfect pose by accident or gravity.  
\*   \*\*Special Trait:\*\* \*\*Elastic Neck.\*\* His neck can contort into impossible curves to avoid danger without him noticing.

\#\# 3\. SCALABILITY (The DNA System)  
\*To create a new character (e.g., A Pig, A Cow), apply the System:\*  
1\.  \*\*Define the Effort:\*\* Is he High Effort (Rooster-like) or Low Effort (Terry-like)?  
2\.  \*\*Define the Shape:\*\* Blocky/Heavy (Pig) vs. Wispy (Insect) vs. Triangular (Aggressor).  
3\.  \*\*Define the Motion:\*\* Does he "Stomp" (On 1s) or "Roll" (On 2s)?
