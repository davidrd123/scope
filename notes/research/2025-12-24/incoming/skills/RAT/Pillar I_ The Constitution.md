\# Pillar I: The Constitution

\> \*\*SCOPE:\*\* The immutable laws of the universe.  
\> \*\*USE WHEN:\*\* Checking logical consistency, resolving conflict, or defining limits.

\#\# 1\. The Ten Laws of Rooster & Terry  
1\.  \*\*Terry Never Attacks:\*\* His victories are accidental. He is a pacifist by nature, a victor by circumstance.  
2\.  \*\*Rooster Defeats Himself:\*\* No outside force defeats Rooster. It is always his own Pride, Impatience, or Invention.  
3\.  \*\*The Motivation:\*\* Rooster \*could\* stop anytime. He chooses not to. He fights for the title of "First Crow."  
4\.  \*\*No Spoken Dialogue:\*\* Characters CANNOT speak words. Speech bubbles and subtitles are \*\*FORBIDDEN\*\*. Text is permitted \*\*ONLY\*\* in two specific forms:  
    \*   \*\*Rooster's Signs:\*\* Wooden placards he holds up (e.g., "Uh oh").  
    \*   \*\*Graphic Onomatopoeia:\*\* Stylized Sound FX text (e.g., "BOOM\!") appearing in-world.  
5\.  \*\*Terry’s Strategy:\*\* He has none. He must never rush, scheme, or panic.  
6\.  \*\*The Boundary:\*\* All action is confined to \*\*The Farm\*\* (Rooftops, Coops, Fields, Fences).  
7\.  \*\*Gadget Materiality:\*\* All machines must be built from "Farm-Punk" materials (Wood, Metal Pails, Rope, Springs). No Sci-Fi.  
8\.  \*\*Gravity is a Choice:\*\* Characters (especially Rooster) fall only when they notice they are floating (Cartoon Physics).  
9\.  \*\*Humiliation \> Pain:\*\* Violence results in Soot, Burnt Feathers, or Flattening—never Blood or Gore.  
10\. \*\*The Sympathy Rule:\*\* We love Rooster because he tries. He is the underdog of his own making.

\#\# 2\. The Physics of Luck  
\*   \*\*Zero-Sum Luck:\*\* The universe is biased. The harder Rooster tries (Effort), the unluckier he gets. The less Terry cares (Apathy), the luckier he gets.  
\*   \*\*The Chuck Jones Axiom:\*\* \`Assumption\` \-\> \`Reality\`.  
    \*   \*The Assumption:\* The Trap will catch the victim.  
    \*   \*The Reality:\* The Trap catches the creator in the most ironic way possible.

\#\# 3\. The "Reset" Law  
\*   No matter the destruction (Explosions, Fired from the Farm, Flattened into a disc), the world \*\*MUST\*\* reset to the status quo at the next Sunrise.
