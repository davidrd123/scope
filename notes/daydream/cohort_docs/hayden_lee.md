Real-2-Avatar
=============

![Image 3: Real-2-Avatar](https://api.daydream.live/v1/assets/resolve?key=assets%2F1766750764304-89dfea31.png)

The goal of this project is to translate human choreography/movement to virtual avatars, with AI-generated backgrounds using StreamDiffusion (which ultimately may lead to AI-generated music videos in the future).
