Hyperbodied is a projection based project utilizing stream diffusion ran locally in touch designer. The images above are my version 1, only based on a still image brought into stream diffusion. I'm still working on integrating mediapipe, pose osc, or kinect into either an FBX file or 2D puppet of myself that will be live tracked from me (or a stand in model/performer). Unfortunately because of physics, the infrared camera was necessary to be able to get better visibility in low lighting due to the projector. I initially tried to utilize pose osc with a normal video camera, then I got the infrared camera, then I tried with just bringing in the video of the person in the morph suit. However, just the morph suit didn't give enough visual information to stream diffusion. I ended up getting a lot of ginger bread - human looking outputs! I also bought a kinect, but I bought a knock off adapter, which doesn't supply steady power (only buy the official adapters!)

There has been a lot of trial and error!

I hope through participating in daydream's cohort this winter 2025, I will be able to have help to finalize the body tracking and optimize stream diffusion to be able to have a fully interactive, live performance.

3.0 Goals include building a website which will live at hyperbodied.com and having a password protected prompt page so that I can do performances in person, as well as in live stream settings. Additional goals are to create video content and post both on the website, @hyperb0died instagram, and publish the philosophic artist statement.
