###

samizdat is an AI-native conceptual editor. You wire it, you visually understand it, it runs.

It exists across three interfaces:

samizdat.ink is the 2D tablet interface where the same surface wires audio signal flow, agent orchestration, NLE sequences, node compositing, workflow automation - whatever domain the moment calls for.

samizdat.computer is the 3D/4D layer where if you can wire it, you're in it - the space reads your presence through whatever TouchDesigner can sense and responds through the full ensemble of ClaudeCode, Blender, TD, and Scope.

samizdat.live is the media stream layer, as interactive as you wire it to be, with Scope serving the realtime AI transformations as new narratives generate, live.
