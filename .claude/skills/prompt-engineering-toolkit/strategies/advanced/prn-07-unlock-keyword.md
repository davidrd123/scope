### **Prompting Strategy: The "Unlock Keyword" Discovery Process**

**Lesson:** The highest level of prompt crafting is not just about describing a scene, but about a continuous, empirical process of **discovering "Unlock Keywords."** These are often high-density, technical, or metaphorical terms that, when combined with a stylistic "Anchor," can reliably produce a complex, desired effect that was not explicitly present in the training data.

**Rationale:** The AI model is not a simple database; it's a complex network of latent connections and capabilities. Many of these capabilities are dormant. Our job as directors and artists is to find the precise combination of words that "unlocks" this hidden potential. This is not a science with a fixed formula, but an art of experimentation. The goal of our iterative process is to build a unique, project-specific palette of these powerful "magic words" that we can then deploy with confidence.

---

### **Few-Shot Example: The Empirical Search for an "Artist-Drawn" 3D Turn**

**The Goal:** To make a 2D character turn in 3D space, but have the motion feel hand-drawn and stylistically authentic, not like a generic CGI render.

* **ITERATION 1 (Basic Command \- Fails):**  
  Surreal Dream Animation — Kai's head turns to the side.  
  * **Result:** The model produces a stiff, unconvincing, or stylistically broken result. The core idea is not landing.  
*   
* **ITERATION 2 (Adding a Technical Vector \- Better, but still generic):**  
  Surreal Dream Animation — The camera does a horizontal orbit around Kai's head, showing its form.  
  * **Result:** The model now understands the 3D motion, but the result looks like a generic 3D model. It has lost the LoRA's "sketchy" style. We have the motion, but not the texture.  
*   
* **ITERATION 3 (Adding a Stylistic Anchor \- Getting Closer):**  
  Surreal Dream Animation — The camera does a horizontal orbit around Kai's head. Preserve sketchy line art.  
  * **Result:** The motion is now starting to look more stylized, but the 3D form feels weak or flat. The AI is struggling to reconcile "2D texture" with "3D motion."  
*   
* **ITERATION 4 (Discovering the "Unlock Keyword"):**  
  Surreal Dream Animation — The camera does a horizontal orbit. \*\*(Anchor)\*\* Preserve sketchy line art while \*\*(Vector)\*\* showing \*\*volumetric form\*\*.  
  * **Result & Discovery:** Success. The high-density, technical term **volumetric form** is the "Unlock Keyword." It provides the AI with a rich, pre-existing concept of how to render shape, mass, light, and perspective. When combined with the "sketchy line art" anchor, it solves the creative paradox, producing a turn that is both dimensional and hand-drawn.  
  * **The Lesson Codified:** We have now discovered a powerful combination. For this project, the phrase "Preserve \[STYLE\] while showing volumetric form" is now a reliable tool in our arsenal for creating stylized 3D rotations. This "unlock" was not known beforehand; it was discovered through a deliberate, empirical process of iteration.
